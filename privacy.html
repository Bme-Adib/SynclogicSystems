Privacy Policy

Our commitment to Digital Sovereignty and Data Ownership.

1. Data Sovereignty

At SyncLogic Systems, we believe that your data is your property. Unlike traditional SaaS providers, our architecture is designed to keep your information within your private ecosystem. We do not sell, trade, or monetize your business data.

2. Information Collection

We collect only the essential information required to provide our engineering services. This includes contact details and technical specifications provided during consultations. Our IoT sensors collect telemetry data strictly for the purposes of monitoring and alerting as defined in your service agreement.

3. Security Standards

We apply biomedical-grade security standards to all stored information. This includes end-to-end encryption for sensitive records and strict access control protocols for our "Secure Business Vault" and AI architectures.

4. Client Rights

You maintain the right to audit your systems and request the complete deletion of your data from our management platforms at any time. We provide tools for you to export your data in sovereign formats.
