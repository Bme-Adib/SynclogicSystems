Engineering standards and operational protocols.

1. Service Engagement

By engaging SyncLogic Systems, you agree to our phased engineering approach. Each project begins with an architectural "Protocol Initiation" to define system requirements and success metrics.

2. System Ownership

Upon final delivery and settlement, the client maintains full ownership of the custom code and infrastructure logic built specifically for their ecosystem. SyncLogic Systems retains rights to proprietary foundational engines used across its architecture.

3. Reliability & Maintenance

While we architect for maximum uptime, the nature of digital infrastructure requires active monitoring. Managed retainers ensure 24/7 telemetry and emergency response. Unmanaged systems are subject to standard support windows.

4. Professional Conduct

We maintain radical transparency in all communications. In return, clients are expected to provide accurate technical data and timely access to hardware assets required for IoT integration.
